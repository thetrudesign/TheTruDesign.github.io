Volunteer

Volunteers are the backbone of our efforts to improve the lives of the animal in our cares.

  • Be at least 18 years old to work with animals
  • Be able to commit to one consistent shift per week for a minimum of 3 months if volunteering in daily shelter operations
Position
Cat Care Volunteer
Be appreciated by our feline friends! This position allows you to assist us in keeping our cat kennels and community spaces in tip top shape. Disinfection and sanitation is of the utmost importance in keeping the cat areas free from potential viral threats. As a Cat Care Assistant you will help to clean, organize, feed, and socialize the animals and ensure the welfare and comfort needs of our feline friends are being met.

Dog Care Volunteer
Be appreciated by our canine friends! As a Dog Care Assistant you will provide our dogs with exercise, enrichment and companionship, and be an integral part in improving their welfare and adoptability.

Adoption Greeter
Be the front line and the face of QAS to potential adopters and the public! Help connect animals and people.

Feral Cat Volunteer

Fundraising Director
Responsible for avenues to solicit donations from businesses and individuals by developing existing and new fundraising products, initiatives and opportunities, build and own key relationships within the community, grant writing and more. If you are interested in this role, please contact our Board President by email at President@quincyanimalshelter.org.
